A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I am a fast learner and usually don't have issues picking up concepts. Sometimes I find it hard to keep on task when I feel like I am ahead and get complacent.
I have a good memory and find it tedious to carry out repetitive practice exercises when I have already grapsed the concept - I prefer to put it into practice and I think the devaademy system encourages this style of learning.

Time management skills. I have already appreciated the suggested use of toggl. I required stronger time management skills at university than in industry and I think devacademy will be a similar experience.
